The story

0 minutes of NPL.
Then everything changed.

At 17, I was playing for West Pymble FC's U18As. Zero NPL. Zero academy. Zero JDL. Eleven straight seasons at the same club — no elite pathway, no SAP, nothing.

The season after, I made my professional debut against Sydney FC's A-League side — sharing the pitch with Andrew Redmayne, Milos Ninkovic, and coach Steve Corica.

My parents never paid a cent of NPL youth fees. I made it in under 2 years. Not by luck — by systems. Meticulously designed development plans that compensated for every session, every game, every level of competition I was missing.

That's Ward Coaching. The exact playbook, built for kids who are behind — and need to catch up fast.
